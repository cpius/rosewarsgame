Here, the Longswordsman can attack the Hobelar or the Archer. First, the normal battle is resolved and it is able to take the tile if it defeats the unit. Then, additional rounds of battles are carried out for additional units it hits. If it attacks the Hobelar, the Catapult and the Archer are also hit. It cant move to these tiles, even if they are defeated. If it attacks the Archer, additionally the Hobelar, Ballista and Light Cavalry are also hit. It cannot hit the Pikeman in this turn.
